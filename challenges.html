FI-WARE/EIT ICT Lab Challenges

An initiative promoted by EIT-ICT Labs

The FI-WARE/EIT ICT Lab Challenges are awarding more than €150.000 to find the most innovative applications based on FI-WARE technologies. The challenge is open only to companies (start-ups and SMEs) and it is articulated in two phases.

During the first phase participants can submit their ideas, using the f6s online form. Then, all the submitted ideas will be evaluated by a independent committee. Finally, the best two ideas will access the second phase. During this phase, the selected teams will receive money prizes up to around €25,000 each, to actually develop the idea. In particular, the second phase has three intermediate checkpoints:

  1. at the first checkpoint the committee will evaluate the final architecture and will release up to €5,000 to each team;
  2. at the second checkpoint the committee will evaluate the prototype and will release up to €10,000 to each team;
  3. at the third checkpoint the committee will evaluate the final application and will release up to €10,000 to each team.

The contract template with term and conditions will be available soon on the web site.
