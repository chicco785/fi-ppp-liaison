FI-PPP Offices co-located with EIT-ICT Lab nodes

The FI-PPP Liaison activity established an initial set-up of the local FI-PPP offices co-located with EIT-ICT Labs nodes. The co-located FI-PPP offices offer a FI-PPP point of presence in the EIT ICT Lab nodes and aims at supporting the engagement of local EIT-ICT Labs ecosystems in the FI-PPP programme.

EIT ICT Lab Nodes

The EIT ICT Labs builds upon Nodes in Berlin, Eindhoven, Helsinki, London, Paris, Stockholm and Trento, leading in ICT, and their partners representing global companies, leading research centres, and top universities.

The role of the Nodes is to execute the strategy of the EIT ICT Labs, focusing on excellence in education, research, and innovation. Based on the best academic and industry researchers, already excellent regional clusters are turned into world-class innovation hotspots. More
